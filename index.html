Beatrix Lestrange

The Journey to Destiny

This Story is inspired by the Sorting Hat Ceremony from the movie Harry Potter & the Philosopher's stone. Beatrix Lestrange is going to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry for the upcoming School Year. She's known as the daughter of a merciless murderer, Bellatrix Lestrange. In Hogwarts, every first-year student shall be sorted to their designated houses by the sorting hat's judgment, but what happens if the sorting hat gives her a situation instead and lets Beatrix make her own choices? Would she still be able to enter Slytherin, where she can make real friends with the cunning folk that uses any means to achieve their ends? Or she might now belong in Gryffindor, where dwell the brave at heart, they're daring, nerve and chivalry set Gryffindors apart; She might also belong in Hufflepuff, Where witches & wizards are just and loyal, those patient Hufflepuffis are true And unafraid of toil; Or perhaps in wise old Ravenclaw, if she has a ready mind, those of wit and learning will always find their kind.

Instructions:

This Story is non-linear, therefore the reader has a choice over where the narrative goes next. Every Choice Has consequences. Pick one amongst all, and you'll prove worthy of them all. Are you ready to get sorted? Let's begin now and get started.
