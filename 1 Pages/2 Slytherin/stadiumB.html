Chapter Three | Sacrifices

fter contemplating, I decided to turn around and help my teammate as fast as I could but to my surprise a group of medical wizards casted a spell that send my teammate floating in the air and landed softly in a cushion-like bed. I ran towards him to check up on him and said

“Captain, Are you okay? Are you hurt? Is ther---” He pushed me to cut me off, he stared at me with raging eyes and said

“You dumb roach! I told you not to lose the snitch. I told you not to lose focus no matter what! Are you stupid?” W-what? I was just trying to help him, and this is his response?

“I was trying to save you and prevent you from hurting yourself. What’s the point of winning if one of us is hurt?” I replied, then He looked at me sternly and said

“NOTHING MATTERS EXCEPT THIS GAME! IF WE LOST, ALL OUR SACRIFICES WOULD BE PUT TO WASTE. STOP MAKING EXCUSES FOR YOUR IGNORANCE AND STUPIDITY.” He picked up his broomstick and continued

“You better pray that you get that snitch and win this game.” I gulped at the sight of him. I am so dead. But why am I to blame? Is it wrong to have concern for your teammates? The game is nothing if one of us would be seriously injured right? I picked up my broom and flew.

Just when I am about to rush back to the game, our opponent got the snitch. The game resulted from 2-3 and they won. I flew back to the stage for the awarding ceremony. The looks that my teammates are giving me send shivers to my bones.

We’re walking back to the quarters when my captain pulled me aside. He deadly stared at me and said

“You are a disgrace to this house, Lestrange. You don’t belong here. Get out. This house does not need losers like you, anyways.” He turned around and walked away. I feel the guilt and embarrassment. I was banished because of my ignorance. Where should I go now?

The End
